Why MessyFox?

Tracking is everywhere and we at MessyFox believe, that REAL privacy is not possible anymore; obfuscation is.

What does MessyFox do?

  • A stripped-down version of Firefox.
  • Poisons your data with automated fake queries/searches.
  • Rotates device fingerprints
  • Fully free and open-source

What does MessyFox NOT do?

  • Does not hide your IP
  • Does not replace a VPN
  • Does not guarantee full anonymity
